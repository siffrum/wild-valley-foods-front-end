Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At Wild Valley Foods, your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to safeguarding the personal information you share with us while shopping for authentic Kashmiri dry fruits, saffron, and related products.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our services:

  • Personal details (name, email, phone number, billing/shipping address)
  • Payment information (processed securely via third-party gateways)
  • Device and browsing data for improving your shopping experience

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used only for:

  • Processing and delivering your orders
  • Improving website performance and user experience
  • Customer support and resolving queries
  • Sending order updates, promotions, and offers (only if you opt-in)

4. Data Security

We use SSL encryption and trusted payment gateways to ensure your personal and financial information remains secure at all times.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Data may only be shared with trusted partners (such as delivery and payment providers) necessary to fulfill your orders.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Request a copy of your stored data
  • Update or correct your personal information
  • Opt-out of promotional emails anytime

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, reach us at: support@valleyfoods.com
